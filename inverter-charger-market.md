<p><p>The inverter charger market can be categorized into three types: pure sine wave, modified sine wave, and split phase. Pure sine wave inverters provide clean, stable power suitable for sensitive electronics, making them ideal for residential and commercial use. Modified sine wave inverters are more affordable but may not be compatible with all devices due to potential distortion. Split phase systems are designed for applications requiring 240V power, commonly used in North American homes, allowing for efficient operation of larger appliances.</p></p>
