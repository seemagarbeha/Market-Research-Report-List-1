<p><p>The Electronic Airlaid Paper market is primarily divided into two bonding types: chemical bonding and thermal bonding. Chemical bonding involves the use of adhesives or bonding agents to enhance the cohesion of fibers within the paper, resulting in a more durable and versatile product. In contrast, thermal bonding utilizes heat to fuse fibers together, offering a lightweight and flexible option. Both types cater to various applications, such as hygiene products, packaging, and industrial uses, emphasizing different characteristics based on bonding methods.</p></p>
