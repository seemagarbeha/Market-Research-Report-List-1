<p><p>The air audit equipment market consists of various types designed to assess air quality and system efficiency. Pressure meters measure static and dynamic pressure within duct systems. Differential pressure meters gauge the pressure difference across filters or other components, aiding in maintenance. Flow meters monitor airflow rates, ensuring optimal system performance. Leak detectors identify air leaks in ducts and insulation, enhancing energy efficiency. Other equipment includes sensors and analyzers that aid in comprehensive air quality assessments across various applications.</p></p>
