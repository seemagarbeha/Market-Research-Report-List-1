<p><p>The pediatric stethoscope market primarily comprises two types: dual head and single head. Dual head stethoscopes feature both a diaphragm and a bell, allowing for versatile sound amplification, which is essential for detecting various heart and lung conditions in children. Single head stethoscopes have a single diaphragm, providing simplicity and ease of use. Both types are designed with smaller chest pieces to suit pediatric patients, facilitating accurate assessments and enhancing the overall effectiveness of pediatric care.</p></p>
