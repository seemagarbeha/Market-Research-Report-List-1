<p><p>Cardiac monitoring and rhythm management devices encompass various technologies crucial for diagnosing and treating heart conditions. ECG devices record electrical activity, providing vital information on heart health. Implantable loop recorders continuously monitor rhythms over extended periods. Cardiac output monitoring assesses heart efficiency, critical for managing heart failure. Defibrillators deliver electric shocks to restore normal rhythm during emergencies, while pacemakers regulate heartbeats in patients with arrhythmias. Additional devices in this market include remote monitoring systems and telemetry devices for ongoing cardiac surveillance.</p></p>
