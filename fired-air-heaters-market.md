<p><strong>The Fired Air Heaters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Fired Air Heater</li><li>Indirect Fired Air Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fired air heaters market comprises two main types: direct fired air heaters and indirect fired air heaters. Direct fired air heaters create heat by burning fuel directly within the air stream, providing rapid heating but potentially affecting air quality. In contrast, indirect fired air heaters use a heat exchanger to separate combustion gases from the heated air, ensuring cleaner air output and improved safety. Both types cater to various industrial applications based on heating efficiency and air quality requirements.</p></p>
