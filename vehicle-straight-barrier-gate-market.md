<p><p>The vehicle straight barrier gate market comprises three main types: wired control, wireless remote control, and automatic systems. Wired control systems are connected directly to a power source and manual controls, ensuring stable operation but limiting mobility. Wireless remote control offers flexibility, allowing users to operate gates from a distance using handheld devices. Automatic systems enhance convenience and security by using sensors or timers to open and close the gate without manual intervention, catering to modern needs for efficiency and ease of use.</p></p>
