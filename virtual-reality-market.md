<p><p>The virtual reality market consists of three main types: non-immersive, semi-immersive, and fully immersive technologies. Non-immersive technology uses standard displays and input devices to create virtual experiences, often found in video games or simulations. Semi-immersive technology blends real and virtual environments, typically using larger screens or projectors, enhancing user experience. Fully immersive technologies provide a complete sensory experience, utilizing headsets and motion tracking to create a realistic virtual world, making it ideal for training, gaming, and entertainment applications.</p></p>
