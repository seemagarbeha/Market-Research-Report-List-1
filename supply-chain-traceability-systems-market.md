<p><p>Supply Chain Traceability Systems encompass various technologies that enable the tracking and monitoring of products throughout the supply chain. RFID tags utilize radio waves for automatic identification, providing real-time data on inventory and location. Barcodes offer a simple, machine-readable graphic that facilitates product identification and tracking at various checkpoints. QR codes, scannable by smartphones, enable quick access to detailed product information. Other technologies may include IoT sensors or blockchain solutions, enhancing traceability, improving efficiency, and ensuring product authenticity.</p></p>
