<p><p>The blood-brain barrier (BBB) drug delivery market encompasses various strategies to enhance pharmacologic access to the brain. The Trojan Horse approach involves utilizing carriers that mimic nutrients to infiltrate the BBB. The Bispecific Antibody Receptor-Mediated Transport (RMT) method employs antibodies designed to bind specific receptors, facilitating drug transport. Increasing permeability focuses on techniques that transiently open the BBB, allowing larger molecules to penetrate. Other innovative approaches include nanotechnology and ultrasound methods aimed at improving drug delivery efficiency to brain tissues.</p></p>
