<p><p>The large-area image sensors market primarily comprises two types: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) image sensors. CMOS sensors are favored for their low power consumption, faster readout speeds, and integration capabilities, making them ideal for applications like smartphones and surveillance cameras. Meanwhile, CCD sensors are known for their superior image quality and sensitivity, often used in high-end cameras and scientific imaging. Both sensor types cater to diverse fields such as healthcare, automotive, and industrial imaging.</p></p>
