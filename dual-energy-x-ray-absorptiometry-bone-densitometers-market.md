<p><p>The Dual-Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (DXA) bone densitometer market features two primary types: axial and peripheral bone densitometers. Axial bone densitometers are designed for measuring bone density at central skeletal sites, such as the hip and spine, providing comprehensive assessments of osteoporosis risk. In contrast, peripheral bone densitometers focus on measuring bone density in the extremities, like the wrist or heel, serving as a quick and portable screening method. Both types are essential for evaluating bone health and guiding treatment decisions.</p></p>
