<p><ul><li>Wing Mounted</li><li>Rear Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airplane engine nacelle market is primarily categorized into wing-mounted and rear-mounted designs. Wing-mounted nacelles are attached directly to the wings, optimizing aerodynamic performance and enhancing lift during flight. This setup is common in commercial jets, where it facilitates easier maintenance access. In contrast, rear-mounted nacelles are positioned at the tail section, which helps reduce cabin noise and improve passenger comfort. This design is often utilized in military aircraft and some regional jets for enhanced stability and performance.</p></p>
