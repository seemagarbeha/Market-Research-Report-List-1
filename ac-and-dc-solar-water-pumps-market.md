<p><strong>The AC and DC Solar Water Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DC Surface Suction</li><li>AC Submersible</li><li>DC Submersible</li><li>AC Surface Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar water pumps market is categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **DC Surface Suction Pumps**: These pumps extract water from a surface source and are powered by direct current, making them ideal for moderate lifting tasks. </p><p>2. **AC Submersible Pumps**: These are designed to operate underwater and utilize alternating current, suitable for deeper water sources, providing efficient water delivery.</p><p>3. **DC Submersible Pumps**: Similar to AC submersibles but powered by direct current, these pumps excel in remote areas with solar energy availability. </p><p>4. **AC Surface Pumps**: These pumps operate above the water level, using alternating current to draw water from shallow sources efficiently.</p></p>
