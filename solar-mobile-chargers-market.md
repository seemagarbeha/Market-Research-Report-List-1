<p><p>The solar mobile chargers market is categorized into two main types: single output and dual output solar mobile chargers. Single output solar chargers provide one USB port, suitable for charging a single device at a time, making them compact and user-friendly for basic needs. Both types harness solar energy, offering eco-friendly charging solutions for mobile devices on-the-go.</p></p>
