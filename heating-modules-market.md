<p><p>The heating modules market primarily comprises two types: metallic and ceramic fiber. Metallic heating modules, made from conductive metals, offer rapid heating and durability, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. In contrast, ceramic fiber modules provide excellent insulation and heat retention, ideal for energy-efficient systems. Each type caters to distinct industrial needs; metallic modules excel in quick heat transfer environments, while ceramic fiber modules are preferred for applications requiring sustained, low-energy heating solutions.</p></p>
