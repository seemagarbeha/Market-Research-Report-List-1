<p><strong>The Antifreeze Testers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Float</li><li>Disc</li><li>Needle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antifreeze testers market comprises three primary types: float, disc, and needle testers. Float testers utilize a hydrometer mechanism to measure the specific gravity of the antifreeze, indicating freeze protection levels. Disc testers feature a floating disc that provides a quick visual indication of coolant concentration. Needle testers use a calibrated needle to show precise readings of antifreeze concentration through a scale. Each type offers unique advantages, catering to different needs in automotive and industrial applications for effective coolant management.</p></p>
