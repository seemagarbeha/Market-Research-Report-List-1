<p><p>The electric air pump market is primarily divided into two types: AC electric pumps and DC electric pumps. AC electric pumps are powered by alternating current and are typically used for stationary applications, offering high efficiency for inflating large items, like air mattresses or inflatable pools. In contrast, DC electric pumps use direct current and are often portable, making them ideal for car use or outdoor activities, such as inflating sports equipment and inflatable toys. Both types cater to distinct consumer needs and applications.</p></p>
