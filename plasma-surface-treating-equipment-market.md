<p><ul><li>Atmospheric Pressure</li><li>Low Pressure / Vacuum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plasma surface treating equipment is classified into two main types: atmospheric pressure and low pressure/vacuum systems. Atmospheric pressure plasma treatment occurs at ambient conditions, effectively modifying surfaces without the need for specialized chambers, making it suitable for continuous processes and large-scale applications. In contrast, low pressure or vacuum plasma treatment operates in controlled environments, allowing for deeper penetration and more uniform treatment, ideal for complex geometries and sensitive materials. Both types enhance adhesion, wettability, and surface cleanliness for various industries.</p></p>
